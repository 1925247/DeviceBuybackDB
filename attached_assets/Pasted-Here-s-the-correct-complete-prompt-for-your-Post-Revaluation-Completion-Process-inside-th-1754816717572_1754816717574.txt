## **📌 Lead Completion Flow (Post-Revaluation)**

### **1. Trigger: After Revaluation**

* Agent completes **Revaluation** step:

  * Updated Q\&A and recalculated **Final Price** saved.
  * Customer confirms acceptance.

---

### **2. Step 1 – Device Photo Upload**

* System prompts **Photo Upload Screen**:

  * Mandatory: **6 photos** of the device.
  * Suggested angles:

    1. Front view (screen)
    2. Back view
    3. Left side
    4. Right side
    5. Top
    6. Bottom / Charging port
  * Validation:

    * All 6 photos required before proceeding.
    * Each photo ≤ 5 MB.
    * Accepted formats: JPG, PNG.
  * Files stored in `lead_photos` table linked to `lead_id`.

---

### **3. Step 2 – KYC Verification**

* KYC form appears after photo upload:

  * Fields:

    * Customer Name (auto-filled)
    * Government ID Type (Dropdown: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving License)
    * ID Number
    * ID Photo Upload (Front & Back if applicable)
    * Customer Selfie (for verification)
  * All KYC data stored in `lead_kyc` table linked to `lead_id`.
  * KYC completion required before payment step.

---

### **4. Step 3 – Payment Mode Selection**

* Agent selects **Payment Method**:

  * Cash
  * Bank Transfer
  * UPI
  * Cheque
* If Bank/UPI:

  * Enter account details or UPI ID.
  * Upload proof of transfer (optional).
* Payment status fields:

  * `pending`
  * `confirmed`

---

### **5. Step 4 – Payment Confirmation**

* Agent confirms:

  * "Payment completed & verified" checkbox.
  * Once confirmed, status changes to `payment_done`.
* Payment details stored in `lead_payments` table.

---

### **6. Step 5 – Complete Device**

* Final step:

  * Agent clicks **"Complete Device"**.
  * Backend updates:

    * `lead_status` = `completed`
    * Completion timestamp logged.
    * All linked data (photos, KYC, payment) locked from edits.
* Confirmation message:

  ```
  Lead #XXXX has been successfully completed.
  ```

---

### **7. Backend & Security Rules**

* All steps must be completed in sequence:

  1. Revaluation
  2. Photos (6 required)
  3. KYC
  4. Payment Confirmation
  5. Complete Device
* If any step is incomplete:

  * Next step button is disabled.
* Once completed:

  * Lead becomes read-only for agents.
  * Admin can view all uploaded documents/photos in backend.
